Octunes help menu

All commands must start with '!'

Commands:
- !play or !pl {link} - Plays song from provided youtube link. Supports timestamps as well.
- !play or !pl {keywords} - Searches youtube based on your keywords and plays first result.
- !skip or !sk - Skips currently playing song.
- !stop or !st - Stops playback and removes songs from queue.
- !queue or !q - Lists the current queue of songs.
- !nowplaying or !np - States the currently playing song.
- !remove or !rm {index} - Removes a song from the queue, use the index provided by the !queue operation.
- !pause or !ps - Pauses the currently playing song.
- !resume or !rs - Resumes the currently playing song, if it is paused.
- !shuffle or !sh - Shuffles the queue. (There must be at least three songs in queue including the currently playing song)
- !bump or !b {index} - Moves a song in the queue to position one, use index provided by the !queue operation.
- !info or !i - Bot info
